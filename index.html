Duck Creek Masonic Lodge November Stated Meeting, Monday, November 13, 2023, 7:00pm.


On October 21, 2023, we held our 50, 60, and 65 Year Service Award Dinner and Awards Presentation. This Dinner was at 6:00pm and was a huge success.


Duck Creek Masonic Lodge is located at 600 N. 5th Street in Garland, TX

Stated Meetings are the second Monday of each Month at 7:00pm

The Duck Creek Education Foundation is a 501c(3) and is accepting donations for school supplies and scholarship awards. Scholarships will be awarded in May of this year, and we continue to collect for the School Supply Drive to support the financially distressed elementary school children within Garland Independent School District. Contact the Lodge at 469-931-2163 for additional information. Click on This Link to donate to the Duck Creek Education Foundation using PayPal.

The Duck Creek Masonic Education Foundation participates in the Grand Lodge of Texas Fantastic Teeth program, and delivered dental care kits to our three adopted GISD Elementary Schools (Left to Right Dorsey Elementary, Classical Center at Vial and Freeman Elementary). Past Master Bo Caperton and Junior Warden Paul Blumhardt delivered these school supplies on September 18 and September 19.
