Duck Creek Masonic Lodge
Holiday Party
Saturday, December 10, 2005

Dear Brothers,

On Saturday December 10, 2005, Duck Creek Lodge hosted a HOLIDAY PARTY to support the Scottish Rite Hospital the Duck Creek Lodge Scholarship fund and the North Texas Food Bank.

The food was GREAT, and the Fellowship was GREATER. PGM Murphey spoke about the current state of Masonry in Texas and why we are doing better and will continue to thrive and grow.
