Award Winning

We’ve labeled our event “award winning” for good reason. The International Special Events Society and Special Events Magazine recognized the 2002 edition of Star Spangled Fourth with the Gala Award for Best/Fair Festival.

 Click here to see the awards and find out more about them.

 

It’s time to “saddle up” for the City of Garland’s award winning Star Spangled Fourth Celebration! 

"It's A Wild, Wild Fourth" 
July 2-6 2003

See what's happening on our Star Spangled Webcam! 

Longhorn steers, roping, bull riding, and everything western will serve as the backdrop for the festivities this year. The event will be held July 2-6. 

We’ll kick things off with a Sneak Preview of the carnival on Wednesday, July 2 from 5:00 p.m. until midnight. The full festival with all the fun of the carnival, the Main Stage, all the daily attractions, and much, much more will run from 4:00 p.m. until midnight July 3; noon to 1:00 a.m. on July 4; and noon until midnight on July 5. Sunday, July 6 will wind down with the carnival open from 4:00 p.m. until 11:00 p.m. 

And as always, admission is FREE!
