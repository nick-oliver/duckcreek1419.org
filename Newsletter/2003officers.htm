On June 28, 2003, Brother Neil Rotzler was installed as Worshipful Master of Duck Creek Masonic Lodge No. 1419. Brother Dale Lucia was installed as Senior Warden, while Brother Woodrow Wetherington was installed as Junior Warden.

Also installed were Brothers Herbert D. Cook as Secretary (big surprise) and Brother John T. McDonald as Treasurer. Also installed were:

Chaplain: Randy Davis
Senior Deacon: Robert Keltner
Junior Deacon: Charles French
Senior Steward: Brian Tedder
Junior Steward: Danny Mladan
Marshall: Nick Oliver
Master of Ceremonies: Woodrow Wetherington
Tyler: Greg Milliorn
