             "Lets hear you shout...  I'm SPECIAL!!!" ...  Together Against Drug Abuse State program coordinator Brother Archie David (Duck Creek Lodge # 1419) told the kids at Centerville Elementary School during the TADA kick-off meeting Thursday,  Nov. 9th;   

       And the  kids responded!       

              In fact, they shouted so loudly      ...    that we had to hold our ears!   Such was the excitement and level of enthusiasm shown by the combined 5th Grand classes of Centerville Elementary in Garland Texas.          

                         As part of the program,  Brother David told of several field trips that the kids would take,  including a day at the Hella Shrine Circus,  a trip to R.W. Judge Boyd Patterson's Dallas courtroom to observe real court proceedings,  and a special lunch, to be served to "Together Against Drug Abuse" programs from around the metoplex at the Scottish Rite Cathedral's Crystal Dining Room.    

    

   

    Garland Police Department Officer Tim Franey, who will be the instructor for the course,  is pictured here with PM Jack Patterson (Duck Creek Lodge) and 5th Grade teachers Ms. Linda Berryhill and Ms. Scottie Horn.                   
 

                

    T-shirts for the program were passed out to each child who would say "I'm Special!"             (they all said it!) and they were told about some of the subjects the course would cover in the next few months.  

                   

          

       Shown seated on stage are: Brother Charles Hinton,  Garland City Attorney (Duck Creek Lodge) Ill. Jack Patterson, PM (Duck Creek Lodge and Garland #441),  Brother Preston Edwards (Garland #441),  Brother Alexander Moreno (Duck Creek Lodge), Brother Bill Grant (Garland #441 and Duck Creek Lodge),  Officer Tim Franey,  Bother Woody Weatherington (Duck Creek Lodge),  Brother Mac Dugger (Duck Creek Lodge),  Brother Robert Standley (APM, Duck Creek Lodge) and his Lady.  Brother Archie David is making the presentation.
