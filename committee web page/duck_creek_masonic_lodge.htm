AUDIT COMMITTEE 

Matt Carter - CHAIRMAN 
Steve Moore
Bo Caperton
Jason Woods
It shall be the duty of this committee, at the close of each Masonic Year, as soon as appointed, to carefully examine and check the reports, books and vouchers of the Secretary and Treasurer, and any other matters referred to them. 
Make a full and accurate report to the Lodge, no later than the August Stated Meeting, showing the condition of the books and accounts. Should any inaccuracies be found, recommend the necessary steps for correction.
The report of the Audit committee shall be read into the Minutes.
To confer with the Long Range Planning committee and Budget Committee in order to 
assist in converting Duck Creek Lodge's present Audit and Budget Bookkeeping system
into a simpler, more streamlined system.
